# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make Learning Management Platform (LMP) a welcoming, respectful, and productive project for everyone.

We expect contributors, maintainers, and users to treat one another with courtesy, patience, and professionalism.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and documentation.
- Assume good intent unless there is clear evidence otherwise.
- Keep collaboration inclusive and focused on learning and improvement.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, insults, or hostile language.
- Spam, trolling, or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

If behavior violates this code of conduct, maintainers may remove content, close issues or pull requests, or restrict participation as needed to protect the project community.

## Reporting

If you need to report a concern, open a private issue or contact the maintainers through the repository owner profile on GitHub.
